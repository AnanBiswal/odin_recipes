Dosa

Recipe for delicious dosa

Dosa tastes good.

  • Dosa batter
  • Masalas
  • Vegetables
  • Chutney
  1. Heat the pan. Spread the batter in a circular shape.
  2. Mix the sliced and saluted vegetables with boiled potatos. Add masalas to it then put the mix on the batter.
  3. Fold the dosa and cook till golden brown. Serve with chutney.
